Who are we?

We are a 2nd year First Tech Challenge (FTC) robotics team this season of 2015-2016. We are from San Diego, California. Before we became an FTC team, we participated in FLL since 2013, until we were old enough to switch to FTC. In 2012 we were fortunate enough to win in the Southern State Championship for FLL and proceed on to the FLL World Championship for the second year. While at the championship we scored sixth in the United States and 13 in the world.

What is FIRST?

FIRST Robotics is a robotics program to catch the interest of young students founded by Dean Kamen, the inventor of the Segway. It is composed of four different programs: Jr. FIRST Lego Legue, FIRST Lego Legue, FIRST Tech Challenge, and FIRST Robotics Competition.
